Cheeseburger Sliders

CHEESEBURGER SLIDERS WITH HAWAIIAN ROLLS

These mini Cheeseburger Sliders with Hawaiian Rolls are packed with tender, juicy ground beef melty cheese, and tangy pickles all smothered in a homemade burger sauce. Perfect for Game Day or family dinner!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup yellow onion, minced
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Spray a 9Ã—13 baking pan with cooking spray and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat, breaking apart the meat while cooking.
  4. While the meat is cooking, add in the minced onion.
  5. Once ground beef is cooked and browned, no longer pink, drain any excess grease and remove from heat.
  6. Add the salt, pepper and garlic powder to the meat, stir to combine.
